Device Reports

Introduction

Reports on device, configuration and link information

Usage

  • Filter on certain values, if desired (won't be taken into account for links to other modules).
  • Select (hold down CTRL for multiple) reports you wish to look at.
  • Adjust the limit to see more or less results.
  • Check 'Alternative' to sort keys rather than values. You see the what's sorted on, on the bottom of each table.

Reports

  1. Distribution of device types
  2. Distribution of operating systems and software versions
  3. Duplicate serial numbers of devices and modules
  4. Duplicate IP addresses of devices
  5. Top PoE budgeds and their usage
  6. CLI devices missing config and configs without changes
  7. Device group and mode statistics (can be VTP related or AP groups in Wlan controllers)
  8. Discover history, where each coloumn is limited individually (use filter to narrow down the timeframe)
  9. Unlinked devices and undiscovered neighbors
  10. Link mismatches based on discovery protocol information
