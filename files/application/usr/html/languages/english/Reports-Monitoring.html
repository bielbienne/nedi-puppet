Monitoring Reports

Introduction

General monitoring statistics like availability and message sources and incidents and how they're acknowledged.

Usage

  • Filter on certain values, if desired (won't be taken into account for links to other modules).
  • Select (hold down CTRL for multiple) reports you wish to look at.
  • Adjust the limit to see more or less results.
  • Check 'Alternative' to sort keys rather than values. You see the what's sorted on, on the bottom of each table.
  • Check 'Optimize' to restrict event statistics to syslog events, with that the source is either a device or node.

Reports

  1. Availability statistics of targets and their locations
  2. List the lastseen, average and maximum latency of targets
  3. List devices with the highest service time
  4. Statistics breakdown of events, their levels and sources
  5. Distribution and duration of cathegorized incidents
  6. Distribution of incidents across targets and their locations
  7. Acknowledge time per user
  8. Incident history in calendar form to "spot patterns" (optimize reveals detailed view)
