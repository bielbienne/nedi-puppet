Invoice Generator

Introduction

It takes a lot of time to work on NeDi. Here's a way to support its development in form of an annual contribution based on the size of your network.

You can enter your address, a comment if you want and select the preferred currency. Click on the print icon when you're done and use the resulting invoice can be printed by clicking on top left icon and used for the payment.
